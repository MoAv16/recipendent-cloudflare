Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Recipendent ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Recipendent, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, profile picture
  • Company Information: Company name, logo, team member details
  • Order/Task Data: Orders, tasks, recipes, folders, notes, and related business data
  • Images: Profile pictures and company logos uploaded through the app

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns, crash reports
  • Authentication Data: Login timestamps, session information

Biometric Data

Recipendent uses Face ID/Touch ID (if enabled by you) to secure sensitive actions like:

  • Changing company logos
  • Modifying company settings
  • Deleting team members

Important: Biometric data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Recipendent service
  • Manage your account and company workspace
  • Enable team collaboration and real-time synchronization
  • Secure your account with Face ID/biometric authentication
  • Send important notifications about your orders and team
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Backend Provider

We use Supabase (a PostgreSQL-based backend service) to store your data securely. Your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Stored in secure data centers
  • Isolated per company (multi-tenant architecture with Row Level Security)
  • Backed up regularly

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication with email verification
  • Role-based access control (Admin, Co-Admin, Employee)
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID)
  • Row Level Security policies to prevent unauthorized access

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With Your Team: Data you create (orders, tasks) is shared with team members in your company
  • Service Providers: Supabase (backend hosting), infrastructure providers
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (contact: recipendent@gmail.com)
  • Export: Download your company data

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days after account deletion
  • Backups: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days

Children's Privacy

Recipendent is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Services

Recipendent integrates with:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo: App infrastructure (Privacy Policy)

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Photos/Camera: To upload company logos and profile pictures (optional)
  • Face ID/Touch ID: To secure sensitive actions (optional, can be disabled)
  • Notifications: To receive updates about orders and team activity (optional)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
